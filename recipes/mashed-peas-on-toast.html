Mashed Peas and Egg on Toast

A quick and healthy breakfast to jump-start your day

Green peas with a hint of feta cheese make this puree irresistible for a well-rounded spring breakfast. Enjoy this healthy spread on a toast with an egg, or as a bright green dip for veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg, poached
  • Slice of bread
  • 125mL green peas
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Cilantro, thinly chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chili pepper powder (optional)

Directions

  1. Place peas in boiling salted water for 1-2 minutes, then rinse with cold water and drain. Mash peas with a bit of olive oil, feta cheese, cilantro, salt and pepper.
  2. Boil an egg for 3-4 minutes. White should be firm but yolk should remain runny.
  3. On a slice of toasted bread place mashed peas and the egg, season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of chili powder.
